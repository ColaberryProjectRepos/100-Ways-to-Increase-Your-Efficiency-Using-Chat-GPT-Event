Explanation of What's Happening


The task at hand is to categorize students in the Colaberry Advanced Interview Prep Bootcamp program into five groups using a combination of metrics with at least three metrics in each classification. The goal is to allow mentors to create weekly email communication to their mentees about their progress. The program is designed to transform students with no experience in the field into job-ready talent and future data leaders. Students are expected to complete the program on time (% On Pace) to hit the market. Students are advised to reach out to Chat GPT or their mentors if they get stuck. The classifications are based on KPIs such as Days on Curr HW, % On Pace, Wks in Program, and Days to Market. The characteristics of each classification are described, and unique advice is given to all groups. The email will be sent on behalf of the IPBC Instructor, and catchy subject lines will be used to get students to read the email.



Email Warning: When this all comes together, the level of detail on the email is amazing.  To think you can produce something like this in just a few seconds is amazing.  While this does save massive amounts of time, you will need to spend some of that time you saved reviewing and validating the information because it is not always accurate so be careful.
